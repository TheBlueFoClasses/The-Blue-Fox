Behind the Brush

The Blue Fox Mission & Concept

Founded in 2024 by three local art advocates, Blue Fox Art Studio was built on a simple premise: Art should come to the community, not hide in formal galleries.

We pack our custom Traveling Art Van with high-grade canvases, German-milled watercolor papers, professional pigment fine-liners, and easel stands, setting up popup workshops in the town's most inspiring corners. From sunlit greenhouse gardens to aromatic coffee roasters, our settings provide the ultimate sensory environment to unchain your inner artist.

Contact Us & FAQ

Have questions about booking private birthday workshops, corporate paint-and-sip popups, or special accessibility accommodations? Drop our team a line directly!

Are all painting materials supplied? ▼

Yes, absolutely! We supply high-grade acrylics, watercolors, pre-stretched cotton canvases, brushes, mixing trays, sketch liners, and a custom protective apron. Simply bring your eager spirit!

What is your booking cancellation policy? ▼

You can self-cancel your registration directly from the "My Bookings" drawer up to 48 hours prior to the session start. This automatically triggers a full ticket credit refund and opens up seats for others on our waiting list.

Can I register on-site at the venue? ▼

Due to strictly limited seating quotas designed to optimize individual mentor attention, we cannot offer walk-in tickets. All class bookings must be reserved and finalized on our online portal.
